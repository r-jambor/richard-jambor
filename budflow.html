BudFlow helps you track expenses, savings and income with ease. A clean interface, smart categories and a practical monthly overview keep your finances clear and in control.

Built from a practical need

I wanted a simple and clean way to track my personal cash flow. BudFlow was created to make it easy to log transactions, organise them by category and understand monthly spending and savings at a glance.

Problem

Tracking personal expenses and savings is often complicated by too many apps and cluttered interfaces and my banking app isn't very user-friendly

Solution

An intuitive iOS app with categorized entries, monthly summaries and a visual dashboard for quick insights.
