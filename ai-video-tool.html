Built to automate a fragmented process

Creating AI video content usually means manually moving between tools for writing, voice generation, visuals and final assembly. This project explores how a single workflow can coordinate those steps into a practical, repeatable system.

Problem

Prompt-based video generation often requires too many disconnected steps, tools and handoffs.

Solution

A modular workflow that chains script generation, voice synthesis, visuals and video assembly into one guided pipeline.
